\aeframe{Training fixed to 10-min episodes: batch=2, 200 steps, configurable from YAML}{
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{OOM fix}: \texttt{batch\_size=16} crashed on 2$\times$H200; fixed to \texttt{batch=2, grad\_accum=2}
    \item \textbf{Timing}: $\sim$2.8s/step $\times$ 200 steps = $\sim$9.3 min training
    \item \textbf{Configurable}: \texttt{max\_steps\_cap} and \texttt{timeout} now in task YAML
    \item \textbf{Tool docstrings}: rewrote all 13 to ``what + when'' only (\srclink{https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.14878}{SAILResearch '26})
  \end{itemize}

  \vspace{0.5em}
  \centering
  Target episode budget: $<$1 min data ops + 10 min train + 5 min eval $\approx$ \textbf{16 min/episode}
}
